Licious sources its fresh produce from trusted partners who operate or manage government-approved slaughterhouses, adhering strictly to the guidelines set by the relevant local or administrative authorities. Licious itself does not engage in the slaughter of animals. Our role involves processing and packaging a variety of meat and seafood products at our state-of-the-art facilities, which are certified with the highest standards of food safety and quality ‚Äî FSSC 22000.

Prawns Cleaning Script

At Licious, during the cleaning process of deveined prawns, only the intestinal tract (sand vein) along the top of the body is removed. The ventral vein, located on the underside of the prawn, is not removed, as it is safe for consumption.

Cancellation Fee Script

We charge a cancellation fee because once an order is placed, we may have already started preparing your shipment or our delivery team could be on the way. Cancelling after the grace period causes disruptions to our operations and affects our delivery partners. It can also lead to food waste. To keep things fair and sustainable, we ask that you only cancel if necessary. We do offer a grace period to make things easier. Thanks for your understanding and support!
